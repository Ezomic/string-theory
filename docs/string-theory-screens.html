The major scale formula

Seven notes, built by stacking a fixed pattern of steps from the root:

W – W – H – W – W – W – H

W = whole step (2 frets), H = half step (1 fret). Start on C and this gives you C-D-E-F-G-A-B-C — all the natural notes.
